About us

Who are we?

The story of Ceramico begins in 2014, in a small clay workshop of just 170 square metres with a team of five people. Despite the many challenges that arose over the years, the enormous effort and tireless dedication of every team member gave birth to a diverse range of ceramic wine bottles. A decade later, the foundation was laid for our own production facility — where today the best team is gathered, united by a shared passion.

The founder and CEO, a professional in advertising by training, never imagined he would one day pursue something so entirely different.

"Working with clay taught me never to give up and to fight to the end for what matters to me."

Our values

The diversity and depth of clay proved enormously captivating — with all its challenges and complexities. It is one of the oldest and most fascinating crafts in human history. Clay has withstood centuries and maintained its uniqueness across the ages. Demand for clay products remains relevant across many fields today, and ceramic vessels hold a particularly special place in Georgian winemaking.

Our production is staffed by an exceptional team. Over ten years we have gained many loyal clients and dear friends. We are proud to collaborate with wine companies that are well known not only in Georgia but beyond its borders. Our products are competitive and can be found on shelves in countries around the world.

Beyond wine vessels, since 2020 we have expanded into new directions — working with white clay, producing beautiful decorative pieces, and taking on individual custom orders. We also produce a wide variety of planters and flower pots, decorating markets and spaces across Georgia.

Clay has an extraordinary power — it strengthens you, energizes you. It taught us to support one another and we became one family. A thing made with love carries a different kind of energy entirely.
